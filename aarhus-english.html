Welcome to SKOR – more than just dart!

Skor is not just an ordinary dart bar. Throughout our venue, you'll find nine dart lanes, each with a tablet and flat screen, where you can choose from several different games and track your results in real-time. Our unique system, which we developed ourselves, makes everything even more fun and easier – three cameras automatically detect where the dart lands and keep track of both turns and points for you. All you need to do is focus on having fun!

As an authentic Icelandic dart bar, we offer a selection of Icelandic drinks, so you can get a taste of Iceland while you play. The story of Skor began quite unexpectedly. It all started when Árni visited a friend in Aarhus. Originally, they were just going to enjoy some open-faced sandwiches and a Carlsberg, but their day took a turn when they stumbled upon the premises here on Telefontorvet. That was the beginning of Skor.
